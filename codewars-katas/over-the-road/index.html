You've just moved into a perfectly straight street with exactly n identical houses on either side of the road. Naturally you would like to find out the house numbers of the people on the other side of the street

The street looks something like this: 1| |6 3| |4 5| |2

Evens increase on the right, odds decrease on the left. House numbers start at 1 and increase without gaps. When n = 3, 1 is opposite 6, 3 opposite 4, and 5 opposite 2;
